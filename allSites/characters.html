Few sites to grab some inspiration while creating characters

  • Pinterest

    A great place to look for inspiration on your characters look. Totally free but with need to register.

  • DeviantArt

    Place to share and watch peoples art and photograph on various topics, including amazing designs of characters. Unlikely to pinterest, DeviantArt focuses mostly only on many types of art, not recipes, sports etc. You can browse it without logging in. Free, paid plan also exist.

  • Fantasy Name Generatos

    Huge generator of names from a lot of categories. Totally free.

  • Character Design References on Pinterest

    Account with amazing collection of character references.

  • Character height comparasion

    Simple tool that helps you compare height of characters.
