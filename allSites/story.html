Tools releated to story planning and executing

  • Story Grid
    In rescources tab we can find many useful articles on various topics. Explaning generes, storytelling, story elements and more.
  • Storyteller roadmap

    Many tips divided into three topics: planning a story, writing it and revising. It's totally free and doesn't require registering.

  • Writing realistic injuries

    Not a good looking site but explains injuries and reaction to them.

  • National Centre for Writing free rescurces

    Site offers free rescources about many stages of your story. You need to complete a short survey before taking them.

  • The Novel Factory: A Premise

    Long article about the central idea of your story and how to find it.

  • The Novel Factory: A Skeleton

    Article to help create the outline of your story, site also offers pdfs for outlines.
