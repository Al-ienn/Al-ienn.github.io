A few podcasts for those who enjoy listening about writing and books

  • Helping writers become authors by K.M.Weiland

    Podcast in english, ongoing since 2009. It has hundreds of episodes. You can hear there everything about books and writing.

  • The Writer's Mind with Tyler Mowery

    Podcast already finished, in english. It's main idea is looking at writing deeper then just structure, while exploring books, philosophies and stories

  • Story Grid Writing Podcast

    Podcas from Story grid about writing. Finished for now.

  • The writing life

    Podcast made by National Centre for Writing. It's about talking to authors about their techniques and writing journeys.
