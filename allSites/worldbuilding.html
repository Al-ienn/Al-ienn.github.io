Useful sites and apps while worldbuilding

  • World Anvil

    World Anvil is a website for worldbuilders overall, not only writers. It has many useful features that will help you creating your world. You need to register to use it, has a lot of features for free.

  • Campfire

    Website for authors that not only let them write and create characters, but also worldbuild. However, many options are limited or locked behind a paywall.

  • Obsidian

    App for taking notes, but also excellent for worldbuilding. If you are confused by it's interface or lost in many features, there are good guides on YouTube. Whole app is free without need to register.

  • r/worldbuilding

    Subreddit for everyone who worldbuild. Discover creations of others, share your own and discuss creating universes.
